# Strategy Research

Research the trading strategy described in the user input.

Produce:
1. **Hypothesis**: the edge you're trying to capture (economic rationale)
2. **Signal**: exact calculation (formula, lookback period, thresholds)
3. **Universe**: which instruments, liquidity requirements
4. **Entry/Exit rules**: clear, unambiguous rules
5. **Risk management**: position sizing, stop-loss, max drawdown limit
6. **Data needed**: OHLCV fields, frequency, history required
7. **Known risks**: when the strategy is expected to fail
