## Requirements

Email verification feature adds ownership confirmation to existing account registration and sign-in flows. After successful account creation, an automated verification email is sent with a cryptographically secure, single-use token that expires after 24 hours. Users must click the verification link to activate their account before accessing protected resources. Unverified users see a non-blocking verification prompt after sign-in. Resend requests are rate-limited to one per 60 seconds. Verification tokens are single-use and immediately invalidated upon successful redemption.
